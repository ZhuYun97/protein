The merit of the proposed method is robust against outliers or heavy-tailed error distributions while asymptotically efficient as the original MAVE under the normal error case. A practical minorization–maximization algorithm is proposed for implementation. Under some regularity conditions, asymptotic distributions of the resulting estimators are derived. Simulation studies and a real data example are conducted to examine the finite sample performance of the proposed method. Both theoretical and empirical findings confirm that our proposed method works very well. The corresponding MM algorithm is also provided. The asymptotic normalities of our proposed estimators are established under some regularity conditions, and the optimal choice of the bandwidth and tuning parameter are introduced by a data-driven procedure.
